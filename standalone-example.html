How to Use in Your Project

Just add these two lines before your closing </body> tag:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="live-css-editor.css">
<script src="live-css-editor.standalone.js" data-auto-init></script>

That's it! The editor will initialize automatically.

Or Initialize Manually

<script src="live-css-editor.standalone.js"></script>
<script>
  // Initialize with custom options
  window.LiveCSSEditor.init({
    autoAnalyze: true,
    initiallyOpen: true
  });
</script>
